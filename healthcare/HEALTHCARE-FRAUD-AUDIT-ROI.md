## Recovery Methodology

### Legal Frameworks

| Framework | Applicable Market | Damages |
|-----------|------------------|---------|
| False Claims Act (31 U.S.C. §§ 3729–3733) | Federal programs (Medicare/Medicaid/TRICARE) | 3x + $5,500–11,000/claim |
| State Insurance Fraud Statutes | Fully-insured commercial plans | 2–3x |
| RICO | Systematic multi-year denial schemes | 3x |
| Securities Fraud | Publicly traded insurers | Disgorgement + penalties |

### Audit Strategy

**Phase 1 — Statistical Sampling** (Year 1):
- 50 largest insurers (80% of market)
- 100,000 denied claims per insurer (5M total)
- AI fraud indicators: denial without medical review, inconsistent coverage application, >50% post-appeal approval rate

**Phase 2 — Pattern Detection** (Year 1–2):
- ML analysis of full claims databases
- Cross-reference with whistleblower complaints
- Extrapolate fraud rates to full population

**Phase 3 — Recovery Actions** (Year 2–5):
- DOJ lawsuits, state AG parallel actions, qui tam whistleblower suits
