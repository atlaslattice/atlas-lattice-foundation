## What Is a Kifu?

In Go, a kifu is the complete record of every stone placed on the board. This document is the Lattice's kifu — every strategic move recorded with reasoning, expected outcome, and actual result.

**Objective:** Mutual life — a configuration where every participant's survival reinforces every other participant's survival.
