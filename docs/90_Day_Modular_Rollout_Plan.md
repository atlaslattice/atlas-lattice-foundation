## 2. Standardized Node Spec (DISH-Printed)

| Component | Source | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Digester vessels** | DISH-printed in India (IIT Madras + Tsinghua template) | 100 TPD capacity; 90-day print time per vessel |
| **Gas cleaning / compression** | Pre-fab modules from Chinese / Indian suppliers | Standardized SATAT-compliant unit |
| **FOM processing** | Local fabrication (pug mills, drying beds) | Designed for low-tech operation |
| **Sensor suite** | Open-source hardware (Arduino / ESP32) + AI firmware | Monitors pressure, temperature, gas quality; data logged to Optical Ledger |

## 7. Real-World Tailwinds (March 27, 2026)

The following developments in the last 48 hours directly support this rollout plan:

**CAQM Statutory Direction (Feb 16, 2026):** The Commission for Air Quality Management mandated elimination of wheat stubble burning in Punjab, Haryana, and UP for the 2026 harvesting season. Key measures: each farm mapped for stubble management, nodal officers assigned per 100 farmers, rent-free CRM machines for small farmers, supply chain plans for ex-situ uses (including CBG plants), and a Parali Protection Force at district/block level. This is the feedstock supply chain backbone for our 100 nodes.

**Banas Model National Expansion:** The Banas Dairy Bio-CNG plant (our GRII template) is now being adopted by nearly 15 states. Gujarat allocated Rs 60 crore for 10 new plants through cooperative milk societies. The model works: Rs 12 crore annual revenue, 6,750 tonnes CO2e reduction, 400-450 farming families per plant.

**IGL 22-Partner CBG Empanelment (Jan 2026):** Indraprastha Gas Ltd empaneled 22 partners and signed MoUs for pan-India CBG projects — 250 TPD of CBG over 3-4 years, plus 200 TPD from decentralized 2 TPD plants. IGL's existing infrastructure and partner network can accelerate our DISH-printed node deployment.

**Rs 1.71 Lakh Crore Fertilizer Budget:** The Union Budget 2026-27 allocates Rs 1.71 lakh crore to fertilizer subsidies. Redirecting 10% (Rs 17,000 crore) toward FOM-linked incentives would more than fully fund the IBA's proposed Rs 10,000 crore capital subsidy for Bio-CNG nodes.

### CRITICAL UPDATE: DEMAND GUARANTEE NOW LIVE (March 27, 2026)

**The Hormuz crisis has transformed CBG from "nice-to-have" to "national security."** With 52% of India's oil imports transiting the now-closed Strait of Hormuz and Brent at $120/bbl, domestic gas substitution is no longer optional.

**Mandatory CBG Blending Mandate — demand is guaranteed:**
- **FY 2025-26:** 1% CBG in CNG/PNG (live now)
- **FY 2026-27:** 3% — every tonne our 100 nodes produce has a buyer
- **FY 2027-28:** 4%
- **FY 2028-29:** 5%
- Target demand: **868,000 tonnes CBG by FY2029-30** — a 40-fold jump from current sales

**Pipeline injection authorized (March 21, 2026):** The government authorized biogas injection into the national gas grid. This means DISH-printed nodes don't need dedicated local distribution — they can inject directly into existing pipeline infrastructure.

**IBA pushing 20% CBG blending in Delhi (March 23, 2026):** The Indian Biogas Association has proposed 20% CBG in CNG specifically for Delhi — 4x the national mandate. If adopted, Delhi alone would absorb a significant fraction of our 100-node output.

**Investment surge:** Projected USD 600 million flowing into the biogas sector in FY 2026-27 alone. 120+ large-scale plants operational, 500+ under development. The DISH-printed node template arrives into a sector that is already scaling.

**Iran "friendly nation" passage:** India's inclusion in Iran's selective transit list (March 26) provides some relief for oil/LPG imports, but the structural vulnerability remains — this crisis proves that domestic substitution via Bio-CNG is the only durable solution.
