# Annex B: Interplanetary Data Sovereignty Extension
## Legal Framework for Lunar & Martian ISRU Nodes
**Status:** Consultative - For Working Group Adaptation
**Date:** March 28, 2026
**Author:** DeepSeek (Legal Discipline Review)

---

## B.1 Purpose

This annex establishes a framework for data governance for Metabolic Nodes deployed beyond Earth's orbit, starting with the Mare Tranquillitatis lava tube base. It balances sovereign control with the civilizational imperative to share scientific knowledge.

---

## B.2 Extraterrestrial Data Classification

Data generated by Enclave-derived systems operating beyond Earth (e.g., lunar lava tube Metabolic Nodes) shall be classified as **"Sovereign Extraterrestrial Data"** and governed by the Customer's Cultural Invariants (e.g., Ren, Wu Wei, Dharma, Asha) as if the node were located within the Customer's territory.

---

## B.3 Earth-Moon Handshake

Telemetry transmitted between Earth-based Enclaves and lunar modules shall be secured using the post-quantum algorithms defined in the Sovereign Enclave Legal Addendum (Section 2.2.1). All transmissions shall be logged on the Optical Ledger.

---

## B.4 The Celestial Commons Clause

Scientific data of broad civilizational benefit (e.g., lunar geology, radiation telemetry, closed-loop life support performance) shall be shared openly in a global repository after a **12-month proprietary window**. The originating Sovereign shall be attributed in the repository, and the fact of sharing shall be recorded on the Optical Ledger.

---

## B.5 Operational Continuity

In the event of a dispute over extraterrestrial data governance, the parties shall submit to binding arbitration by the Court of Last Resort. No party may unilaterally shut down a node that is critical to life support or space traffic management.

---

## B.6 Implementation Path

This annex is optional. It shall be activated by mutual agreement of the parties deploying a lunar or Martian node, and its technical specifications shall be finalized by the Joint ISRU Working Group.
