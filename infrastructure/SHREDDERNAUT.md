# Shreddernaut v1.0 — Distributed Waste Processing Architecture

**Status:** Concept Ready for Pilot Deployment | **Date:** January 10, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Self-contained, modular waste processing unit deployed at major landfills and municipal transfer stations. Converts incoming plastic waste into Trashium feedstock 24/7. Eliminates long-haul transport, creates constant local collection → processing → value loop.

**51% worker equity from day one.**

---

## Technical Specifications

### Physical Configuration
- **Standard Unit:** 40-ft high-cube shipping container
- **Throughput:** 50–150 tons/day
- **Uptime Target:** 95%
- **Operating Range:** -20°F to 120°F

### Zone Layout (40-ft Standard)
- **Zone 1 (0–12 ft):** Intake & AI sorting (hyperspectral cameras, magnetic/density separators)
- **Zone 2 (12–24 ft):** Shredding & washing (2-stage rotary, hot water rinse, spin dry)
- **Zone 3 (24–36 ft):** Pelletizing & storage (3–8mm chips, 10-ton buffer silos)
- **Zone 4 (36–40 ft):** Power & controls (edge compute node, switchgear, backup port)

### AI Sorting System
- **Hardware:** NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin (275 TOPS), hyperspectral NIR cameras
- **Performance:** 30 fps real-time, 98% plastic type identification
- **Resin separation (premium):** HDPE/LDPE/PP at 95% purity → 2–3x higher sale price
